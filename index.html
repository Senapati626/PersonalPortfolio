state of covid 2021

A conclusive audit of who's who and what's what regarding everything COVID. Here, you can check daily updates of number of deaths, cases and recoveries globally with world leaders in each categories in a bar chat format. You can search for any nation and their related data along with beautiful line charts showcasing the increase or decrease in relevant statistics across the year. Theme and concept inspired by State of JS.

beetlenut solutions

A wholesome Beetlenut finding app. One can login as an user, shop owner or site administrator. Users can check details of all beetlenut shops present in their inputted PIN code while shop owners and administrator will get real time update on which user searched for a beetlenut store in their location.
